## Questions 1–10: ADR, limitation, pre-action, jurisdiction, service

### Q1. Court-ordered mediation

A technology supplier brings a £75,000 contractual claim. The customer refuses mediation solely because it believes that a court has no power to require participation in a non-court process. The judge considers that a short mediation would be proportionate, would not impair either party’s right to a judicial determination, and might narrow the issues.

What is the best statement of the court’s power?

A. The court cannot order or encourage mediation without both parties’ consent.  
B. The court may stay proceedings for a proportionate non-court dispute resolution process that does not impair the essence of the right to a judicial hearing.  
C. The court may permanently dismiss the defence because mediation was refused.  
D. The court may compel the customer to settle but cannot require attendance at mediation.  
E. The court can consider mediation only after disclosure.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** `Churchill v Merthyr Tydfil CBC [2023] EWCA Civ 1416`에 따라 proportionate하고 재판접근권의 본질을 해치지 않는 court-ordered ADR/stay가 가능하다.

**Elimination:** A는 Halsey를 절대명제로 오해한다. C·D는 participation과 settlement 강제를 혼동한다. E와 같은 단계 제한은 없다.

### Q2. Arbitration agreement

A distributor commences High Court proceedings concerning payment obligations that fall squarely within a written arbitration clause. The manufacturer has taken no step in the proceedings to answer the substantive claim.

What should the manufacturer normally do first?

A. File a full defence and then ask the arbitrator to set aside the court claim.  
B. Apply to the court for a stay under section 9 of the Arbitration Act 1996.  
C. Ignore the proceedings because the arbitration clause automatically nullifies the claim form.  
D. Apply for summary judgment on the merits.  
E. Serve a Part 36 offer.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** 유효한 arbitration agreement 범위의 claim이면 실체적 대응 전에 s 9 stay가 정상적 조치다.

**Elimination:** C처럼 court proceeding이 자동 무효가 되지는 않는다. A는 substantive step으로 stay right를 위험하게 만들 수 있다. D·E는 forum 문제보다 뒤다.

### Q3. Simple contract limitation

A buyer paid for machinery on 1 March 2019. The seller was required to deliver it on 1 April 2019 but failed to do so. The buyer discovered in June 2025 that the seller had never ordered the machinery. No fraud or concealment is alleged. The buyer wishes to issue a breach of contract claim on 15 July 2026.

What is the best advice?

A. The claim is in time because six years runs from discovery.  
B. The claim is in time because no loss arises until the buyer discovers the failure.  
C. The claim is prima facie time-barred because six years ran from breach on 1 April 2019.  
D. The claim has a 12-year period because machinery is a capital asset.  
E. The court must disapply limitation if the breach was deliberate.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** Limitation Act 1980 s 5: simple contract는 cause of action, 즉 breach에서 6년. 발견시점이 일반적으로 기산점을 미루지 않는다.

**Elimination:** A·B는 latent negligence와 혼동. D는 deed 여부이지 자산 성격이 아니다. E는 자동 재량이 아니다.

### Q4. Personal injury knowledge

A laboratory worker suffers a respiratory injury caused by exposure at work. Symptoms began in 2021, but only a specialist report received on 10 May 2025 linked the significant condition to the employer’s process. The worker issues on 1 May 2027.

Which provision is most directly relevant to whether the claim is within the primary period?

A. Limitation Act 1980 s 5 only.  
B. Limitation Act 1980 ss 11 and 14, comparing accrual with the claimant’s date of knowledge.  
C. Limitation Act 1980 s 14A only.  
D. A fixed six-year period from exposure.  
E. A 15-year long-stop that applies to every personal injury claim.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** PI는 s 11의 3년, accrual 또는 s 14 date of knowledge 중 늦은 날. 사실관계상 2025 knowledge가 핵심이다.

**Elimination:** s 14A는 PI가 아닌 latent damage negligence. E는 s 14B long-stop을 잘못 확장한다.

### Q5. Protocol non-compliance

A claimant issues a professional negligence claim without sending a letter of claim, although limitation was not close to expiring. The claimant has otherwise pleaded an arguable case.

What is the most likely procedural consequence?

A. The claim is automatically void.  
B. The court must enter judgment for the defendant.  
C. The court may stay the claim, give directions, and impose costs or interest consequences.  
D. The claimant commits contempt merely by issuing.  
E. The limitation period is restarted.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** pre-action non-compliance는 통상 discretionary case management/costs consequence다.

**Elimination:** A·B·D는 자동 제재를 과장한다. E는 limitation law와 무관하다.

### Q6. Correct court

A company has a straightforward £42,000 debt claim. There is no public importance, specialist issue, or need for an unusual High Court remedy.

Where should it normally start proceedings?

A. Supreme Court.  
B. Court of Appeal.  
C. High Court because every company claim belongs there.  
D. County Court.  
E. Employment Tribunal.

**Answer: D**

**Rule/Application:** 단순한 £100,000 미만 money claim은 정상적으로 County Court에서 시작한다.

**Elimination:** company status가 High Court 관할을 만들지 않는다. A·B는 appellate courts, E는 고용관계가 아니다.

### Q7. Part 7 or Part 8

Trustees seek the court’s interpretation of a clause in a trust instrument. All relevant documents are agreed and no substantial dispute of fact is expected.

Which procedure is most suitable?

A. Part 8 claim.  
B. Part 20 counterclaim.  
C. Default judgment.  
D. Part 71 information order.  
E. A criminal information.

**Answer: A**

**Rule/Application:** substantial factual dispute 없이 document construction/declaration이 중심이면 Part 8이 적합할 수 있다.

**Elimination:** 나머지는 추가청구, 무응답판결, 집행정보, 형사절차다.

### Q8. Claim form service within jurisdiction

A claim form is issued on 12 February. It is to be served within England and Wales. No extension is obtained.

By when must the claimant normally complete the relevant step required by CPR 7.5?

A. Within 14 days of issue.  
B. Within 28 days of issue.  
C. Within four calendar months after issue.  
D. Within six calendar months after issue.  
E. At any time before trial.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** within jurisdiction는 issue 후 4 calendar months. 6개월은 일반적으로 service out.

### Q9. Email service

The claimant emails the claim form to a solicitor on the final day for service. The solicitor had corresponded by email but had never indicated that email could be used for service and had not confirmed authority to accept service.

What is the best analysis?

A. Any prior email correspondence constitutes automatic consent to email service.  
B. Service is necessarily valid because the solicitor opened the email.  
C. The claimant faces a serious validity problem and may need an order validating alternative service, if a good reason exists.  
D. A claim form may never be served electronically.  
E. The defendant must apply for default judgment.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** PD 6A 동의·address 및 solicitor authority 요건을 확인해야 한다. actual receipt만으로 자동 유효는 아니다(`Barton`). CPR 6.15 order 가능성은 good reason에 달린다.

**Elimination:** A·B는 동의요건을 삭제한다. D는 절대금지 오류. E는 party를 뒤바꿨다.

### Q10. Separate particulars

A claimant serves a claim form on the defendant. The particulars of claim were not included or served with it.

What is the normal deadline for serving separate particulars?

A. Seven days after issue, regardless of service.  
B. Fourteen days after service of the claim form, but never later than the final date for serving the claim form.  
C. Twenty-eight days after acknowledgment of service.  
D. At disclosure.  
E. Seven days before trial.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** CPR 7.4의 두 겹 제한을 모두 기억한다.

## Questions 11–20: response, statements, interim remedies, tracks, sanctions

### Q11. Defence timetable

Particulars of claim are deemed served on a defendant on 2 June. The defendant files a valid acknowledgment of service within 14 days and does not challenge jurisdiction. No extension is agreed.

When is the defence normally due?

A. Seven days after deemed service.  
B. Fourteen days after deemed service.  
C. Twenty-eight days after deemed service.  
D. Twenty-eight days after filing the acknowledgment.  
E. Three months after issue.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** 적시 AoS는 defence를 particulars deemed service 후 28일로 늘린다. D는 기산점을 바꾼 함정이다.

### Q12. Jurisdiction challenge

A defendant served in England believes the English court lacks jurisdiction. It files an acknowledgment of service indicating an intention to dispute jurisdiction.

What must it normally do next to preserve that challenge?

A. File a merits defence within 14 days and wait for trial.  
B. Make the CPR 11 application within 14 days after filing the acknowledgment.  
C. Ignore the claim form.  
D. Apply to an arbitrator even though there is no arbitration agreement.  
E. Seek default judgment.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** AoS 표시만으로 충분하지 않고 CPR 11 application timetable을 지킨다.

### Q13. Default judgment filed too late

The defendant’s defence was due on Monday. It was filed on Wednesday morning. The claimant requests default judgment on Wednesday afternoon. No default judgment had been entered before the defence was filed.

What is the best result?

A. Default judgment must be entered because the defence was late.  
B. The request should generally fail because a defence was filed before default judgment was entered, though sanctions/costs may remain.  
C. The defence is automatically struck out forever.  
D. The court loses jurisdiction.  
E. The claimant must discontinue.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** CPR Part 12의 entitlement는 request/entry 시점의 filed response를 본다. lateness는 별도 case management 문제다.

### Q14. Mandatory set aside

Default judgment was entered even though the defendant had filed a defence within the applicable time before judgment was entered.

Which rule applies?

A. The court must set aside the wrongly entered judgment under CPR 13.2.  
B. The defendant must prove exceptional circumstances under CPR 13.3 only.  
C. Judgment can never be set aside.  
D. The defendant’s only remedy is an appeal to the Supreme Court.  
E. The claimant decides whether it remains valid.

**Answer: A**

**Rule/Application:** wrongly entered default는 mandatory set aside. correctly entered default의 discretionary set aside와 구별한다.

### Q15. Defence pleading

A defendant knows that an allegation is false because a different event occurred. Its draft defence states only: “Paragraph 8 is denied.”

What is the best advice?

A. A bare denial is always sufficient.  
B. The defendant should give reasons for the denial and state its positive version where appropriate.  
C. The defendant should omit any response.  
D. The defendant should put all evidence in the claim form.  
E. The defendant must admit the allegation and challenge it at trial.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** CPR 16은 deny 이유와 다른 version을 요구한다. A·C는 allegation이 deemed admitted 또는 poorly pleaded될 위험.

### Q16. Part 18 request

A defendant serves a 40-page request asking for every item of evidence the claimant may rely on, although the pleaded issues are already clear.

What is the best response?

A. Part 18 is a device for unrestricted evidence fishing.  
B. The request is inappropriate to the extent it is not reasonably necessary and proportionate to clarify matters in dispute or obtain needed information.  
C. Every Part 18 request automatically becomes a court order.  
D. Part 18 can be used only after trial.  
E. Refusal automatically ends the claim.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** clarity/necessary information과 proportionality가 목적이다. disclosure나 interrogatory 전면대체가 아니다.

### Q17. Strike out or summary judgment

A defence sets out a legally recognisable defence, but the claimant says the available evidence makes success merely fanciful. There is no allegation of abuse or pleading non-compliance.

Which application most directly addresses this contention?

A. Summary judgment under Part 24.  
B. Witness summons under Part 34.  
C. Charging order.  
D. Part 71 order.  
E. Appeal.

**Answer: A**

**Rule/Application:** no real prospect + no compelling reason for trial은 summary judgment. pleading 자체가 no reasonable grounds/abuse라면 strike out.

### Q18. Interim injunction

A former agent threatens to publish confidential source code tomorrow. There is a serious issue to be tried. Damages would be difficult to quantify. The claimant is financially able to give a cross-undertaking.

What is the most relevant framework?

A. Denton relief test.  
B. American Cyanamid: serious question, adequacy of damages, cross-undertaking and balance of convenience.  
C. Criminal standard of proof.  
D. Part 36 costs test.  
E. Default judgment.

**Answer: B**

### Q19. Interim payment

The defendant has admitted liability for a serious injury. Quantum will be tried next year, and the claimant is plainly likely to recover a substantial sum. The claimant needs funds for immediate rehabilitation.

What is the most suitable application?

A. Security for costs.  
B. Interim payment under Part 25.  
C. Third party debt order.  
D. Summary assessment of costs.  
E. Appeal by way of case stated.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** admission과 substantial final award 전망이 gateway. amount는 reasonable proportion이어야 한다.

### Q20. Track allocation

A £68,000 contractual claim is between one claimant and one defendant. Properly managed, the trial should last two days and each party needs one expert. There is no excluded category or unusual complexity.

Which is the normal track?

A. Small claims track.  
B. Fast track.  
C. Intermediate track.  
D. Multi-track is mandatory for every claim above £25,000.  
E. No track applies to contract claims.

**Answer: C**

**Rule/Application:** £100,000 이하, trial 3일 이하, expert/party 제한과 비례적 관리가 가능하면 intermediate가 정상 후보.

### Q21. Relief from sanctions

A party serves a witness statement one day late because of a diary error. Trial is six months away, no hearing is lost, and no prejudice beyond minor inconvenience arises. The party applies immediately for relief.

Which approach should the court take?

A. Refuse automatically because every breach is fatal.  
B. Apply Denton: seriousness/significance, reason, and all circumstances including efficient litigation and compliance.  
C. Decide only whether the underlying claim is strong.  
D. Apply the criminal burden of proof.  
E. Enter default judgment without application.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** 사소함·promptness는 relief에 유리하지만 자동 결과는 아니며 3단계 전부 적용.

## Questions 22–30: evidence, disclosure, privilege, costs, appeal, enforcement

### Q22. Civil hearsay

A company wishes to rely on an email written by a former employee who now lives abroad and will not attend trial. The email is relevant and authentic.

Which statement is best?

A. Hearsay is automatically inadmissible in civil proceedings.  
B. It may be admissible under the Civil Evidence Act 1995, subject to notice, possible cross-examination steps, credibility and weight.  
C. It is conclusive proof because it is written.  
D. It can be used only if the opponent agrees.  
E. It becomes privileged merely because litigation has begun.

**Answer: B**

### Q23. Standard of proof

A claimant alleges a serious but civil fraud. The judge considers the event inherently unlikely.

What standard applies?

A. Beyond reasonable doubt because fraud is serious.  
B. Balance of probabilities; inherent improbability affects evaluation of evidence, not the legal standard.  
C. Clear and convincing evidence as a separate English standard.  
D. No burden applies.  
E. The defendant must disprove fraud.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** `Re B`—민사기준은 하나. allegation seriousness가 별도 standard를 만들지 않는다.

### Q24. Expert’s duty

A client tells its engineering expert to omit a test result that undermines the client’s case because the client is paying the expert.

What should the expert do?

A. Follow the instruction because the retainer overrides all other duties.  
B. Remain independent and comply with the overriding duty to help the court, addressing material contrary information.  
C. Ask the solicitor to rewrite the opinion without attribution.  
D. Destroy the result before disclosure.  
E. Become the client’s advocate at trial.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** CPR 35와 Ikarian Reefer. 비용지급자가 결론을 통제하지 않는다. C·D는 professional misconduct/contempt 위험.

### Q25. Standard disclosure

Which document falls most clearly within standard disclosure even if the party does not intend to rely on it?

A. A document that materially undermines that party’s own pleaded case.  
B. A wholly irrelevant personal diary.  
C. A document outside the party’s control with no relevance.  
D. Every document ever created by an employee.  
E. Only documents favourable to the disclosing party.

**Answer: A**

**Rule/Application:** CPR 31.6은 adverse documents와 상대를 support하는 documents 포함.

### Q26. Disclosure and inspection

A party lists a solicitor’s confidential advice on the dispute and states that inspection is withheld for legal advice privilege.

Which statement is correct?

A. Listing the document necessarily waives privilege.  
B. Disclosure and inspection are distinct; the document may be listed while inspection is withheld on a valid privilege claim.  
C. Privileged documents must be destroyed.  
D. Privilege belongs to the solicitor alone.  
E. The court must inspect every privileged document automatically.

**Answer: B**

### Q27. Litigation privilege

After adversarial litigation is reasonably contemplated, a solicitor asks an independent surveyor to investigate an accident. The dominant purpose of the report is obtaining evidence and advice for that litigation.

What is the best analysis?

A. It can never be privileged because the surveyor is a third party.  
B. It is capable of litigation privilege because litigation is contemplated and the dominant purpose test is met.  
C. It is legal advice privilege solely because a surveyor wrote it.  
D. It is without prejudice because it concerns an accident.  
E. It is automatically public.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** litigation privilege는 lawyer-client 외 third party evidence도 보호 가능. legal advice privilege와 구별.

### Q28. Part 36 defendant’s offer

A defendant makes a valid Part 36 offer. The claimant rejects it and proceeds to trial but obtains a judgment less advantageous than the offer. It would not be unjust to apply the normal consequences.

What is the normal costs position after expiry of the relevant period?

A. The claimant automatically receives indemnity costs.  
B. The defendant normally receives its costs from expiry of the relevant period, with interest on those costs.  
C. Each party must bear all its own costs.  
D. The claim is treated as never having existed.  
E. The defendant must pay the claimant an additional amount.

**Answer: B**

**Rule/Application:** claimant가 D offer를 못 이기면 CPR 36.17 defendant-side consequence. A·E는 successful claimant offer의 결과와 혼동.

### Q29. Appeal and stay

A losing defendant files an appellant’s notice within time. The claimant starts enforcement of the judgment.

What is the best advice to the defendant?

A. Filing the appeal automatically stays enforcement.  
B. The defendant should apply for a stay because an appeal does not automatically stay enforcement.  
C. Enforcement can never occur while permission is pending.  
D. The judgment ceased to exist on filing.  
E. Only the claimant may ask for a stay.

**Answer: B**

### Q30. Enforcement against a bank account

A judgment creditor knows that a bank holds £35,000 in an account belonging beneficially to the judgment debtor. The judgment remains unpaid.

Which enforcement method most directly targets that asset?

A. Third party debt order.  
B. Attachment of earnings.  
C. Witness summons.  
D. Search order.  
E. Part 36 offer.

**Answer: A**

**Rule/Application:** bank가 debtor에게 진 debt를 interim/final third party debt order로 묶는다. B는 wages, C는 evidence, D는 pre-judgment evidence preservation, E는 settlement.
