# SQE1 Day 1 Diagnostic Lesson

## Objective

Diagnose whether the candidate can convert legal knowledge into SQE1-style client application.

## Lesson sequence

1. Explain the FLK1 and FLK2 split.
2. Explain why ethics appears across subjects.
3. Teach the SBA elimination method.
4. Run a short mixed diagnostic.
5. Classify errors.
6. Convert errors into rule-sheet tasks.

## Diagnostic prompts

| Area | Diagnostic task |
|---|---|
| Business | Choose the correct business medium for liability and control facts. |
| Contract | Decide whether a term is incorporated and what remedy follows. |
| Dispute Resolution | Identify the next civil procedure step. |
| Land | Decide whether an interest binds a buyer. |
| Wills | Identify validity or distribution issue. |
| Ethics | Identify conflict, confidentiality, undertaking, or court-duty issue. |

## Output after lesson

The candidate should have:

- a weak-subject list;
- an error-code profile;
- a first revision schedule;
- a personalised rule-sheet backlog.
