# SQE1 FLK1 Tort Law package

Status: in progress

## Deliverables

- Korean lecture-style theory textbook (minimum 100 rendered A4 pages)
- English original SBA workbook with full answer and distractor explanations
- DOCX and PDF editions

## SRA scope map

1. Negligence: duty of care
2. Breach of duty and standard of care
3. Factual causation
4. Legal causation and remoteness
5. Defences, including contributory negligence and consent
6. Remedies and assessment of damages
7. Psychiatric harm
8. Pure economic loss and negligent misstatement
9. Employers' liability
10. Vicarious liability
11. Occupiers' Liability Acts 1957 and 1984
12. Product liability and Consumer Protection Act 1987
13. Private nuisance and public nuisance
14. Rylands v Fletcher

## Mandatory case treatment

Every material case must include:

- material facts
- legal issue
- court's decision
- rule or principle established
- how the point is likely to operate in an SQE single-best-answer question

## Quality gates

- no repeated long paragraphs
- no cloned scenarios
- no keyword-only fragments
- no empty self-test templates
- sequential Part numbering
- all five SBA options tailored to the facts
- no predictable answer-position pattern
- no file paths, timestamps, or generation metadata in the body
- render and inspect every page before release
