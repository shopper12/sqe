# Lawful Source Map

This project must be built from official and lawful public materials. Do not copy proprietary textbooks, paid prep course notes, paid question banks, leaked questions, or pirated PDFs.

## Primary Official Sources To Monitor

- Solicitors Regulation Authority (SRA): SQE assessment rules, qualification rules, SQE2 exemption guidance, character and suitability, admission requirements.
- SQE Assessment / Kaplan SQE: SQE1 assessment specification, sample questions, booking windows, fees, assessment dates, seat availability, candidate guidance.
- UK legislation: legislation.gov.uk for statutes and statutory instruments.
- UK courts and official judiciary resources for public judgments and procedural materials.
- Government and regulator resources where relevant, including GOV.UK, HMCTS, Companies House, Land Registry, FCA, ICO, CMA, and professional conduct materials.

## Secondary Lawful Sources

- Public university explainers and open legal education materials.
- Public legal information sites, only where checked against primary sources.
- Original notes and question drafts created in this project.

## Source Rules

1. Prefer official sources when an exam rule, date, fee, process, or legal standard may have changed.
2. Use primary legal materials for black-letter law whenever practical.
3. Clearly distinguish settled rules from exam strategy or inference.
4. Do not reproduce substantial copyrighted passages.
5. MCQs must be original, not copied or paraphrased from paid banks.

## Monitoring Targets

Check these regularly before relying on old notes:

- SQE1 booking opening and closing dates.
- SQE1 assessment dates and locations.
- SQE fees.
- SQE1 assessment specification updates.
- SQE2 exemption application guidance for foreign-qualified lawyers.
- SRA admission and character/suitability requirements.
