## Day 1 이론: English Legal System 핵심 10개

1. England and Wales는 하나의 jurisdiction으로 SQE의 기준법이다.
2. 법원 구조는 대체로 first instance, appeal, Supreme Court의 위계로 이해한다.
3. Binding precedent는 상급법원의 ratio decidendi가 하급법원을 구속하는 원칙이다.
4. Obiter dictum은 설득력은 있을 수 있으나 binding rule은 아니다.
5. Statute가 common law보다 우선한다.
6. Courts interpret statutes using interpretative approaches and aids.
7. Civil matters와 criminal matters는 목적, 당사자, 입증기준이 다르다.
8. Civil standard는 balance of probabilities다.
9. Criminal standard는 beyond reasonable doubt로 설명된다.
10. Ethics and professional conduct는 별도 과목처럼만 나오지 않고 모든 FLK 문제에 숨어 들어간다.

## Day 1 미니 문제

### Q1

A junior solicitor is asked by a partner to send a client letter immediately. The junior notices that the letter contains a statement which is probably misleading, but the partner says that it is "commercially necessary" and tells the junior not to worry. What is the best response?

A. Send the letter because the partner is responsible.  
B. Send the letter but keep a private note.  
C. Refuse to send it unless the misleading statement is corrected.  
D. Send it because client interests override all other duties.  
E. Ask the client whether they accept the risk.

정답: C

해설: SRA duties and ethics are personal. A solicitor must not mislead and cannot hide behind a partner's instruction. Client interests do not override duties to the court, administration of justice, and integrity.

### Q2

Which statement best describes the role of a binding precedent?

A. Any statement made by any judge must be followed.  
B. The ratio decidendi of a higher court may bind a lower court in later cases.  
C. Obiter comments always bind future courts.  
D. A statute is invalid if inconsistent with precedent.  
E. A lower court can ignore a higher court if the lower court considers the rule unfair.

정답: B

해설: Binding precedent concerns the ratio decidendi, not every judicial statement. Statute takes priority over inconsistent common law.

### Q3

In a civil claim, what is the usual standard of proof?

A. Beyond reasonable doubt.  
B. Clear and convincing evidence.  
C. Balance of probabilities.  
D. Absolute certainty.  
E. Prima facie suspicion.

정답: C

해설: Civil claims generally use the balance of probabilities standard. Criminal proceedings use the higher criminal standard.
